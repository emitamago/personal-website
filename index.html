Hi, my name is Emi Tsukuda software engineer
who love solve problem, challenge, make things better

I am originally from Tokyo, came to US 2001 to attend school. Graduated Business Administration and entered jungle of adulthood.
After couple jobs, I settled in wild Restaurant industry. Started as a server in small mom and pop restaurant at Reno. I climb ladder quite lots. From small restaurant server to GM at well known San Francisco restaurant. I think I did good.

Beside my busy restaurant life, I randomly found online coding class . It was beginner Ruby class. I don't know why but I started to take the online course. My first struggle was install Ruby to my machine . It took me whole day to figure out. But when I finally installed Ruby,
I felt undescribable excitement and joy. I think I fell in love with coding at the moment.

Busy life prevented to continue to study coding for while. But back of my mind, coding was alway there. It came up to my mind time to time, and asking me "hey, when you finally admit that you can't run away from me???"

Winter 2018, I finally made up my mind. I admitted to accept love of my life. I researched options for me to learn coding. There are many options, well too many options. But I choose to attend coding bootcamp. I found it is the best way for me. Again, research many bootcamp and finally found the best bootcamps for me. Rithm School

Days at Rithm was nothing but fun. I was having too much fun and often I caught myself smiling on my way to Rithm. I learn many many things everyday. Instructors were extremely helpful and knowledgeable. Now I am graduating school and can't wait to see my next journey!

So many technologies in this wild world. There are so many different features. But I strongly believe they all stand on common ground, logical problem solving basic. I will continue to learn this filed and I am very grateful that learning is part of my life.
